Destinations
POPULAR TOUR LOCATIONS
Sigiriya Rock Fortress
A 5th-century ancient fortress built on a massive rock column, Sigiriya features frescoes, landscaped gardens, and an awe-inspiring view. It’s also referred to as the “Lion Rock” due to the giant lion paws carved at the entrance.
Temple of the Tooth
Located in Kandy, this temple houses the sacred tooth relic of Lord Buddha. It’s a revered pilgrimage site for Buddhists and a UNESCO World Heritage Site with traditional Kandyan architecture.
Yala National Park
Known for the highest leopard concentration in the world, Yala also shelters elephants, sloth bears, and bird species. Visitors enjoy safaris across its varied ecosystems.
